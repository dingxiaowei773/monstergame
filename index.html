About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character.

With intuitive controls and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment as you battle through hordes of enemies, unlock new characters, and discover powerful combinations of abilities.

Play directly in your browser - no downloads required! Test your skills and see how long you can survive in this challenging apocalyptic world.

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Space to use special ability (when available)
  • E to pick up items and power-ups
  • ESC to pause the game

Tips for Beginners

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Prioritize health pickups when your health is low
  • Experiment with different character abilities
  • Focus on upgrading a few weapons rather than spreading upgrades thin
  • Learn enemy patterns to predict their movements
